# Generative AI Interfaces

## Role in AIG-NCII: Creation

Contributes to the non-consensual creation of AI-generated intimate images.

## Description

Generative AI Interfaces increase the accessibility of AIG-NCII by providing an easy-to-use
interface (e.g., computer software, website, or mobile app) for users to access generative AI model capabilities. 

In the 2010s face-swap era, there were already [undressing](https://www.vice.com/en/article/deepnude-app-creates-fake-nudes-of-any-woman/) apps like [DeepNude](https://regmedia.co.uk/2019/10/08/deepfake_report.pdf)
that had over 95k active users. However, in the 2020s with the advancement of generative
AI technology, **AI nudifier applications** became a rapidly growing [multi-million](https://indicator.media/p/ai-nudifiers-continue-to-reach-millions-and-make-millions) dollar economy
dedicated to the creation and [monetization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.09751) of AIG-NCII, [predominantly](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/10778012251397966) of young women. AI nudifier applications significantly lower the barrier to entry to creating AIG-NCII
because any non-technical user can upload a photo of another person (like a [yearbook photo](https://www.404media.co/ai-porn-secret-desires-chatbot-face-swap/) or
social media post) and create an “undressed” version of that person within minutes without
their consent. The apps offer varying [features](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.09751) including undressing and positioning the subject
in various sexual positions. 

In addition to single-purpose AI nudifier applications, **general
purpose model interfaces** have also been used to produce AIG-NCII. WIRED [found](https://www.wired.com/story/google-and-openais-chatbots-can-strip-women-in-photos-down-to-bikinis/) that ChatGPT
and Gemini have been used to “strip women in photos down to bikinis.” Most notably, Grok
has been used to generate [thousands](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-01-07/musk-s-grok-ai-generated-thousands-of-undressed-images-per-hour-on-x) of images of women and girls directly into the comment
section of X. As AI companies begin adding “spicy” and
“erotica” modes into their chatbot products, [advocates](https://rainn.org/groks-spicy-ai-video-setting-will-lead-to-sexual-abuse/) are also sounding alarm bells for the
predictable proliferation of AIG-NCII.
