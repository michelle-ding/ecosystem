# Deepfake Creation Communities

**Role in AIG-NCII: Proliferation & Discovery**

Contributes to the proliferation and discovery of technologies used to non-consensually create and distribute of AI-generated intimate images.

**Description**

Deepfake Creation Communities are online communities that provide general and technical
assistance to members trying to create deepfakes. They are a “[key driving force](https://regmedia.co.uk/2019/10/08/deepfake_report.pdf) behind the increasing
accessibility of deepfakes and deepfake creation software” and serve as an “entry point” for new
users to learn from experienced users. 

Historically, [“deepfake creation communities”](https://www.tsjournal.org/index.php/jots/article/view/126) have
been large (reaching 100,000 members) and highly mobile (existing across platforms like Reddit,
MrDeepFakes, 4chan, 8chan, Voat, Telegram, and Discord). In 2017, [journalists](https://www.theverge.com/2018/2/7/16982046/reddit-deepfakes-ai-celebrity-face-swap-porn-community-ban)
uncovered “r/deepfakes” on Reddit, one of the earliest deepfake creation communities with over
90,000 subscribers that focused on face swapping celebrity faces with porn performers. In February, 2018, under public pressure, Reddit [banned](https://www.reddit.com/r/announcements/comments/7vxzrb/update_on_sitewide_rules_regarding_involuntary/) “r/deepfakes” (and other threads like “
r/CelebFakes”) and updated their site-wide rules “against involuntary pornography and sexual or
suggestive content involving minors.” After the “r/deepfakes” ban, many members migrated
to MrDeepFakes.com (MDF), the self-proclaimed “largest deepfake community,” which serves as [both](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.11100) a deepfake video distribution and consumption site and a forum for deepfake content
creators. MDF was also linked directly by the popular face-swap model “DeepFaceLab” on Github as a place to obtain [technical support](https://www.tsjournal.org/index.php/jots/article/view/126) to use the model. Most recently, [Telegram](https://www.404media.co/inside-the-telegram-channel-jailbreaking-grok-over-and-over-again/) has been a “deepfake creation community” for users using Grok AI to generate AIG-NCII.
