# Critical Service Providers

**Role in AIG-NCII: Infrastructural Support**

Provides critical infrastructural support that allows technologies on the creation and distribution levels to function.

**Description**

Critical Service Providers, including cloud service providers, domain name services (DNS),
and authentication services, are support software, enabling the existence of multiple pieces of the
technological ecosystem, including nudifier apps and deepfake websites. 

An Indicator [audit](https://indicator.media/p/ai-nudifiers-continue-to-reach-millions-and-make-millions) of 85 AI nudifier websites found that “Amazon and Cloudflare provide hosting or content delivery services for 62 of the 85 nudifiers” and “Google enabled simple sign-on for 53 out of 85.” On May 6, 2025, Mr.DeepFakes, the world’s most notorious “deepfake pornography” site with over 650,000 users [shut down](https://www.npr.org/2025/05/06/nx-s1-5388422/mr-deepfakes-porn-site-ai-shut-down) because an unknown “critical
service provider terminated service permanently” and “data loss has made it impossible to continue
operation.”
