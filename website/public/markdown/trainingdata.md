# Training Data

**Role in AIG-NCII: Creation**

Contributes to the non-consensual creation of AI-generated intimate images.

**Description**

Training datasets refer to text, image, video, audio, and/or multi-modal datasets used to train generative AI models. 

In the early 2010s face-swap models were trained using open-source face datasets and other curated NSFW datasets comprised primarily of women. An early [study](https://regmedia.co.uk/2019/10/08/deepfake_report.pdf) of the 2019 DeepNude undressing app found that it was unable to generate images of men because it was trained only on images of women. Researchers have found that “general-purpose” image datasets like [LAION-5B](https://purl.stanford.edu/kh752sm9123),  [ImageNet](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.16923), and [LAION-400M](https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.01963) also contain pornography, non-consensual intimate images, and/or known CSAM, enabling models that train upon them to generate AIG-NCII.
